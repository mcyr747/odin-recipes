Pizza

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Yeast
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Mix the flour, water, yeast, and salt together.
  2. Knead the dough for 10 minutes.
  3. Let the dough rise for 1 hour.
  4. Roll out the dough and top with tomato sauce, cheese, and pepperoni.
  5. Bake for 15 minutes at 475°F.
