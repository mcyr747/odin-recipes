Burger

Burger Ingredients

  • Bun
  • Patty
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Lettuce
  • Condiments

Steps

  1. Gather all of your ingredients
  2. Place the patty on the grill
  3. When patty is almost done cooking, place buns on grill to toast
  4. Take buns off of grill and assemble burger
  5. Place patty on burger and enjoy
